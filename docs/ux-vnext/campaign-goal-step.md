# CCX-403 Campaign goal step

Goal captures Campaign name, founder-facing Campaign type, desired outcome, an optional related Partner program or product, and owner. Supported types are Partner outreach, Customer re-engagement, and Announcement. Type changes guidance only; they never alter safety standards or sending authority.

The projection restores saved nested Campaign draft fields, filters related records through current visibility rules, and uses the CCX-402 scoped expected-version save plan. Validation errors are field-specific and connected through `aria-describedby` and `aria-invalid`. No internal engine names are rendered.
