Use the isolated persisted seed described in `docs/ux-vnext/demo-walkthrough.md`. This is application data exercised through real scoped APIs, not a static mockup. Sending and publishing remain off.

## Opening

Open `http://127.0.0.1:3001/#today`.

Say: “LegalEase turns today’s operating decisions into controlled Social, Outreach, Partner, and Files workflows—with truth carried all the way into investor readiness.”

## The five primary destinations

1. **Today:** open the Fulton kickoff action, create a synthetic follow-up, reload, and show the saved item.
2. **Social:** open `demo-post-review`, show the exact creative reference and independent variants, make a harmless draft edit, save, and reload. Point out the hard publishing gate.
3. **Outreach:** open the county-intake campaign, show exact audience and suppression counts, save wizard progress, and stop before launch. Approval and execution remain separate.
4. **Partners:** open Fulton County, show the immediate next action and links into Outreach and Files, update the internal next action, and reload.
5. **Files:** find and preview the compliance memo, show its relationships and access state, then show why Investor Room readiness excludes draft, stale, failed, and missing records.

Close: “This is not a slide deck about an operating loop. These are persisted records, scoped writes, exact links, and fail-closed external actions.”

If a workflow cannot save and survive reload, use the approved screenshots only to explain the intended direction and disclose the limitation. Never present a screenshot as live product behavior.
